How I
Think

A systematic approach to creative problem solving

Core Philosophy
Great design solves problems, not just aesthetics. Every decision must serve a purpose.
Less is always more. Remove until what remains cannot be taken away.
Systems enable creativity. Structure liberates, it doesn't constrain.
Process
Step 01

Understand

Deep dive into the problem space. Research users, market, and context. Define what success looks like before touching any design tool.

Step 02

Define

Distill chaos into clarity. Identify core problems, constraints, and opportunities. Create a strategic framework that guides every design decision.

Step 03

Design

Execute with precision. Balance form and function, beauty and utility. Iterate rapidly, validate constantly, refine relentlessly.

Step 04

Systemize

Build for scale. Create frameworks and systems that outlast individual projects. Document decisions so others can build upon this foundation.

Design is not
what it looks like.
Design is how it works.
